## 1. Executive Summary

Two families of ideas, born in the same milieu (the Bay Area of the 1980s-90s), structure the imaginary of radical tech:
- **transhumanism** (surpassing the biological limits of the human through technology);
- **crypto-anarchy / cypherpunk** (using cryptography to remove the individual from the power of the state).

They **share a root** (libertarianism, techno-utopian faith, the same people) but **diverge on the end goal**: transhumanism wants to **transform the human** (a *vertical* project, sometimes collectivist/longtermist); crypto wants to **emancipate the sovereign individual** (a *horizontal* project, anti-state). In 2020-2026, they **converge partially** within the Thiel/Andreessen orbit, e/acc and the "network state" — while retaining strong tensions (notably e/acc **against** effective altruism).

---

## 2. The Transhumanist Pole

**Genealogy:**
- **Extropianism**: **Max More** & Tom Morrow launch *Extropy Magazine* (1988) then the **Extropy Institute** (1992). A **libertarian** doctrine: "boundless expansion," "self-transformation," "dynamic optimism," "spontaneous order."
- **World Transhumanist Association** (1998), founded by **Nick Bostrom** & David Pearce → becomes **Humanity+** (2008).
- **Ray Kurzweil**: *The Singularity Is Near* (2005), the Singularity predicted for **~2045** (human/AI fusion, the end of disease and death). Co-founds **Singularity University** (2008) with Peter Diamandis, sponsored by Google/NASA.
- **Nick Bostrom**: *Superintelligence* (2014), the **existential risk** of AI → the matrix of longtermism.
- **Eliezer Yudkowsky**: rationalism (LessWrong), AI safety.

**Central idea:** technology should serve to **improve/surpass the human** (genetic engineering, nanotech, AI, *mind uploading*, cryonics). Internal tension: between an **optimistic/accelerationist** wing (More, Kurzweil) and a **cautious/safety-focused** wing (Bostrom, Yudkowsky) obsessed with existential risk.

---

## 3. The Crypto / Cypherpunk Pole

**Genealogy:**
- **Cypherpunks**: **Eric Hughes, Tim May, John Gilmore** found the mailing list (1992). *A Cypherpunk's Manifesto* (Hughes); *The Crypto Anarchist Manifesto* (May): strong cryptography makes the individual **invisible to the state**.
- **Forerunners of Bitcoin**: **Nick Szabo** (bit gold, smart contracts), **Wei Dai** (b-money), **Adam Back** (Hashcash), **Hal Finney** (RPOW, the first recipient of a Bitcoin transaction).
- **Satoshi Nakamoto**: Bitcoin (2008-2009), a synthesis of these works.
- **Balaji Srinivasan**: *The Network State* (2022) — theorizes **"voice vs exit"**: rather than reforming a failing system (*voice*), **leave** it to build a new one (*exit*), with Bitcoin being "the ultimate exit technology."

**Central idea:** to remove the **individual and value** from state control (money, contracts, identity) → **individual sovereignty** and, ultimately, **new political entities** (network states).

---

## 4. The Historical Welding Point

The **decisive** fact: **the same people** were in both camps. **Hal Finney, Nick Szabo and Wei Dai** — the inventors of crypto — belonged to the **transhumanist/rationalist networks** (Extropians, Bay Area mailing lists). Hal Finney was a notorious extropian (and was cryopreserved). Crypto **is born within the transhumanist ecosystem**: these are not two alien worlds, but **two branches of the same libertarian-techno-utopian tree**.

## 5. Where It MERGES (Convergences)

1. **A common libertarian root.** Distrust/hostility toward the state, faith in spontaneous order and individual exit.
2. **Techno-utopianism.** Technology solves fundamental human problems (death, scarcity, coercion).
3. **Shared personnel.** Finney/Szabo/Dai literally link the two. Thiel funds crypto **and** anti-aging/biotech. a16z has a mega crypto fund **and** invests in **AI/longevity**.
4. **"Exit."** *The Sovereign Individual* is the **bridge text**: it speaks to the crypto crowd (monetary exit) **and** to the transhumanists/network-staters (new sovereignties).
5. **e/acc as a common roof.** Effective accelerationism (Andreessen) aggregates the two: accelerating AI (the transhumanist pole) **and** deregulating crypto/finance (the cypherpunk pole) under a single anti-brakes slogan.
6. **Pro-natalism & longevity.** A recent convergence: defeating death (transhumanism) and "50 billion inhabitants" (anti-Malthusianism) come together in an imaginary of **expansion**.

---

## 6. Where It DIVERGES (Fault Lines)

1. **Vertical vs horizontal.** Transhumanism = **transform the human** (a project on the species). Crypto = **protect the individual** as they are (a project on power). One can want one without the other.
2. **Collective vs individual.** **Longtermism** (a wing of transhumanism) reasons in terms of **aggregated future humanity** (billions of potential lives) — a quasi-**collectivist/utilitarian** logic. Crypto-anarchy is **radically individualist**. A real philosophical friction.
3. **e/acc AGAINST effective altruism (EA).** The most visible rift. **EA/longtermism** (Bostrom, MacAskill, and the former icon **Sam Bankman-Fried**) advocates **caution** in the face of the existential risk of AI → hence **brakes**. **e/acc** (Andreessen, Beff Jezos) sees in this a "decelerationist" betrayal and wants to **accelerate**. Both descend from transhumanism but **clash** over AI. The collapse of **FTX/SBF** (Dec. 2022) discredited EA and **boosted e/acc**.
4. **Decentralization vs concentration.** Crypto promises **decentralization**; yet cutting-edge AI (compute, data, capital) is **ultra-centralized** (OpenAI, a16z, hyperscalers). The "accelerated AI" project in practice contradicts the crypto ethos. An unresolved tension.
5. **AI safety.** Yudkowsky/Bostrom ↔ accelerationists: should one **slow down** or **charge ahead**? This is the fault line running through *all* of the milieu.
6. **Relationship to the state.** Classic cypherpunks = **anti-state** on principle. But the 2025-2026 tech-right (Thiel, Palantir, Anduril) **merges with the state** (defense contracts, government posts). The original cypherpunk would see this as a **betrayal**.

## 7. TESCREAL: The Map of Acronyms

The acronym **TESCREAL** (coined by **Timnit Gebru** & **Émile P. Torres**) groups together:
**T**ranshumanism · **E**xtropianism · **S**ingularitarianism · **C**osmism · **R**ationalism · **E**ffective **A**ltruism · **L**ongtermism.

- **Gebru/Torres's thesis:** these are not separate ideas but an interconnected **"bundle,"** sharing origins (eugenics, an instrumental definition of "intelligence") and **authoritarian implications**. They analyze it as a **quasi-religion** of Silicon Valley that **justifies costly/risky projects in the name of a grandiose future** ("if the stakes are extinction or 10^58 future lives, anything is permitted").
- **Counter-critique:** some transhumanists (e.g. **James Hughes**, IEET — a left-wing *techno-progressive* current!) contest the conflation: TESCREAL would lump together families with opposing politics (from the neoreactionary far right to transhumanist socialists). The acronym is **useful as a map, debatable as a blanket attribution of intent**.

> Key takeaway: TESCREAL is **the best available map** of the ideological cloud, provided one does not turn it into a monolithic bloc. The **internal fault lines (e/acc vs EA, accel vs safety) are as important as the convergences**.

---

## 8. Synthesis

**It merges** on the **root** (techno-utopian libertarianism), the **personnel** (Finney/Szabo, Thiel, a16z), the **bridge text** (*The Sovereign Individual*) and the **e/acc roof**.
**It diverges** on the **end goal** (transforming the species vs emancipating the individual), the **relationship to the collective** (utilitarian longtermism vs crypto individualism), and above all on **AI** (accelerate vs secure — the **e/acc vs EA** war).

The real movement of 2025-2026 is a **right-wing synthesis**: the **accelerationist + individual-sovereigntist** wing, allied with **state power** (Thiel/Palantir/Vance), provisionally prevails over the **cautious/longtermist** wing (weakened by the fall of SBF). But the **accelerate vs secure** fault line remains the **seismic fault** that could recompose everything if a major AI accident occurs.
