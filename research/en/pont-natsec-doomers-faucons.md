## 1. Leopold Aschenbrenner: the doomer turned hawk turned hedge fund

The pivot figure the dossier was missing. German, ~24 years old, formerly of OpenAI's Superalignment team (fired in 2024), tied to FTX's Future Fund.

- **"Situational Awareness: The Decade Ahead"** (June 2024, 165 pages): AGI around 2027, superintelligence around 2030, and above all Part IV, "**The Project**": de facto nationalization of the labs predicted for 2027-28, a core team in a secured SCIF-type site, a trillion-dollar cluster funded by Congress. Quote: "I find it insane that the US government would let a random SF startup develop superintelligence."
- **The documented semantic inversion**: of the 7 occurrences of the word "existential" in the essay, **3 refer to the race with China, only 1 to AI risk for humanity** ("Against Aschenbrenner" analysis, EA Forum). The existential threat has changed object: it is no longer AI, it is Beijing. The entire rhetorical apparatus of safety (urgency, irreversibility, "we only get one chance") is retained, but flipped into an argument for acceleration.
- **The monetization**: in late 2024, simultaneously with publication, Aschenbrenner launched the hedge fund **Situational Awareness LP**: ~$225M at the start, **more than $1.5B under management by October 2025** (verified). Investors: the Collison brothers (Stripe), Nat Friedman, Daniel Gross. Positions: long energy (Vistra, Constellation, Bloom Energy) and compute (CoreWeave), short chipmakers. In Q1 2026, the 13F showed ~$13.7B of notional exposure against an estimated real cash AUM of ~$383M: massive leverage. *(AUM/notional distinction: medium confidence, primary filing not consulted.)*
- **Reading for the dossier**: the apocalypse as an investment thesis. Aschenbrenner sits at the exact intersection of Part 11 (he comes from EA/OpenAI safety) and Part 8 (he becomes a capital manager): "caution" converted into a long-infrastructure financial product. It is the purest proof that the accelerate/slow-down axis does not map onto the axis of power.

## 2. AI 2027: the scenario Vance read

- **"AI 2027"** (April 2025, AI Futures Project): Daniel Kokotajlo (ex-OpenAI, left in 2024 refusing the non-disparagement agreement), with Scott Alexander. A scenario of superhuman AGI in 2027 leading by 2030 either to utopia (from the American point of view) or annihilation. **More than a million readers within weeks, including JD Vance.**
- Kokotajlo himself has since lengthened his horizon (November 2025: "around 2030, lots of uncertainty") and clarified that late 2027 was only his mode (~40%), not his median.
- The most structured critique: **Vitalik Buterin** (July 10, 2025), the scenario assumes a quasi-divine attacker facing static defenders, never modeling a world that defends itself (cf. d/acc, Part 19).
- **The political function exceeds the intention**: Kokotajlo is a sincere doomer, but his scenario, written as a US-China race, feeds the very race narrative he wants to ward off. Same mechanics as the Yudkowsky-Soares book blessed by the security establishment (Part 14): **in the field of 2025-26, any narrative of imminent AGI is convertible into a rearmament argument, whatever its explicit conclusion.** *(Direct causal influence on the AI Action Plan remains an inference, not a documented fact.)*

## 3. The death of the safety summit: Bletchley → Paris → New Delhi

The trajectory of the international summits is the chronicle of a semantic slide:

| Summit | Name | Signal |
|---|---|---|
| Bletchley Park, Nov. 2023 | AI **Safety** Summit | Declaration signed by 28 countries including the US and China |
| Seoul, May 2024 | AI Summit | Voluntary commitments from the labs |
| Paris, Feb. 2025 | AI **Action** Summit | **Vance's speech**: "The AI future is not going to be won by hand-wringing about safety"; US and UK refuse to sign; China signs |
| New Delhi, Feb. 2026 | AI **Impact** Summit | Kratsios (White House): "**We totally reject global governance of AI**"; the "frontier commitments" no longer mention coordination |
| Geneva, 2027 (announced) | ? | First UN forum on AI scheduled for July 2026; whether a safety dimension survives is uncertain |

Three days after Paris, the **UK AI Safety Institute was renamed the "AI Security Institute"** (same acronym, withdrawal of work on bias, refocus on cyber/CBRN). On June 3, 2025, the **US AISI became CAISI** ("Center for AI Standards and Innovation"), refocused on national security and "adversarial AI." **The slide Safety → Action → Impact / Security → Standards is the major political fact of the period: the word "safety" was administratively eliminated from the international vocabulary in 18 months.**

## 4. Trump policy 2025-2026: deregulation by decree

- **EO 14179** (January 23, 2025): repeal of Biden's EO 14110 (and its 10²⁶ FLOPs reporting threshold).
- **"America's AI Action Plan"** (July 23, 2025): 24 pages, 90+ actions, designed by **David Sacks** (AI/crypto czar, Part 2) and the OSTP. Three pillars: accelerate, build the infrastructure, dominate AI diplomacy. Slogan: "Build Baby Build." Accompanied by the EO "Preventing Woke AI in the Federal Government."
- **The failed moratorium**: the House version of the "One Big Beautiful Bill" (May 2025) contained a **10-year federal moratorium on state AI laws**, removed by the Senate on **July 1, 2025 by a 99-to-1 vote**. A spectacular, bipartisan failure.
- **Revenge by decree: EO 14365** (December 11, 2025): creation of an "**AI Litigation Task Force**" at the DOJ tasked with suing states over their AI laws, and conditioning $42B of federal funds (BEAD) on the withdrawal of "onerous" regulations. Legally contested (a decree does not preempt a state law; DeSantis publicly critical).
- **USCC recommendation** (November 2024): a "Manhattan Project-style program" for AGI. Max Tegmark: "**suicide race**." RAND proposes an Apollo model. Concept to retain: "**soft nationalization**" (Convergence Analysis), no frontal seizure of the labs, but an accumulation of levers (contracts, security, funding) until the state/labs boundary blurs.

## 5. The Anthropic affair: safety designated a threat to the state

February 2026, the most revealing episode of the period:

- The Pentagon-Anthropic negotiations collapse: Anthropic demands contractual guardrails (no mass surveillance of Americans, no lethal autonomous weapons).
- Trump orders the agencies to stop using Anthropic; Defense Secretary **Hegseth designates Anthropic a "supply-chain risk to national security"** (February 27, 2026), the first time an *American* company has received this designation, until then reserved for firms from adversary countries.
- **The same day, OpenAI signs** a contract worth up to $200M accepting "any lawful purpose."
- Anthropic sues the administration; federal judge Rita Lin **blocks the designation on March 26, 2026** (retaliation contrary to the First Amendment).

**Reading**: the "supply-chain risk" designation turned against an American company for maintaining ethical red lines. Safety is no longer merely ridiculed (Paris); it is **punished as disloyalty**. To be filed under Part 13: this is a marker of fascization in McQuillan's precise sense (Part 19), the subordination of scruples to raison d'État.

## 6. Military AI in actual use: Gaza, Ukraine

The dossier had the "Authoritarian Stack" (Palantir, Anduril); here is its documented application:

- **Gaza** (investigations by +972 Magazine / Local Call, **Yuval Abraham**): "The Gospel" (Nov. 2023, up to 100 targets/day versus ~50/year manually); "**Lavender**" (April 3, 2024): up to **37,000 Palestinians listed** as potential targets, ~10% error rate admitted, human validation in **~20 seconds** ("rubber stamp"), and the "Where's Daddy?" system for striking at home. All figures verified against the primary source.
- **Ukraine**: onboard AI raises the strike rate of FPV drones from 30-50% to ~80% (Auterion/Skynode S, works in GPS-denied environments); first fully robotic attack (drones + UGVs, no soldier on the ground) near Lyptsi, December 2024. On the Russian side: the V2U drone running on NVIDIA Jetson Orin, consumer chips inside weapons.
- **The labs cross the line**: OpenAI removes its military-use ban (January 2024), **OpenAI-Anduril** partnership (December 2024, counter-drone); **Anthropic-Palantir-AWS** (November 2024, Claude in intelligence work); CDAO contracts of July 2025: up to $200M each for xAI ("Grok for Government," 6 days after the MechaHitler episode, Part 18), Google, OpenAI, Anthropic. **Project Maven** (Palantir): ceiling raised to ~$1.3B (May 2025), Army objective: "one thousand high-quality targeting decisions in one hour."
- **A genuine zone of uncertainty**: the actual level of lethal autonomy. Analysts maintain that a human remains "in the loop"; the +972 testimonies (20 seconds of validation) suggest the loop is a procedural fiction. Undecidable from open sources, and that is precisely the problem.

## 7. Verdict

1. **The bridge exists and it has a name**: natsec doomerism / "AGI Realism." Its ideological function is to convert safety's rhetorical capital (existential urgency) into a program of militarized acceleration. Aschenbrenner is its theorist-financier, AI 2027 its involuntary narrative vector, the USCC its institutional translation.
2. **2025-2026 marks the total victory of this bridge**: summits renamed, institutes renamed, repeal EOs, a moratorium attempted by both routes (legislative: 99-1 failure; executive: ongoing), safety designated a national-security risk. The caution camp of Part 11 did not lose a debate; it lost the state apparatus.
3. **For the dossier's grid**: axis 1 (accelerate/slow down) is dead as an operative cleavage, everyone is accelerating. What remains is axis 2 (concentration/democracy), and on that axis the period documents: mass algorithmic targeting in Gaza, "any lawful purpose" contracts, the punishment of scruples, the preemption of local laws. Part 13 sought a working definition of techno-fascism; this part supplies its operational chronicle.
