## 1. Framing the Stakes: The Fascist Rhyme Is Functional

The frame must be set at the outset, otherwise the part becomes "tech bros are macho" and that is unfalsifiable. Historical fascism was natalist and virilist in a constitutive way: Mussolini's "Battle for Births" (Battaglia per la natalità, 1927), the Nazi Lebensborn, the cult of the male body and of motherhood-as-duty. The reproduction of the "good" population and heroic virility were at the heart of the project, not on its margins.

So when, in 2026, we find a pronatalism coupled with genetic selection and an anti-feminine virilist aesthetic, we are not observing an atmosphere, we are observing **a structural rhyme**, that is, the same function (to reproduce and rank a population) in a new vocabulary. That is what makes this part load-bearing for the dossier's thesis.

## 2. The Demographic Fact

The leadership of the entire dossier is almost entirely male: Thiel, Musk, Andreessen, Altman, Karp, Sacks, Vance, Yarvin, Land, Aschenbrenner, Hoffman, Luckey, Huang, Amodei. Women in real power are rare and often in a second or exceptional position (Daniela Amodei, president of Anthropic; Mira Murati; Fei-Fei Li). In the dossier, women appear above all as **critics**: Hao, Crawford, Gebru, Zuboff (Part 16). This asymmetry is not anecdotal: it structures who thinks the world in this milieu and who contests it.

## 3. Pronatalism as State and Class Ideology

Pronatalism is no longer a whim, it is a policy with relays in power.

- **Elon Musk** is its mouthpiece: he has declared "demographic collapse" a greater fear than climate change, and he has more than ten known children, with several women, including a Neuralink executive.
- **Simone and Malcolm Collins** are its public face and the bridge to eugenics (Part 28). At the second **Natal Conference** in Austin (2025), the mood was "exuberant," buoyed by the hope of allies in the administration (Musk, Vance). Simone cultivates a "techno-puritan" aesthetic embraced as "intentionally cringe." They select their embryos by polygenic scores.
- **The coalition** brings together three usually distinct factions: the religious right, the "tech types," and the anti-feminists of the "new right." They diverge on IVF and genetic engineering but agree on the idea that modern culture has devalued the nuclear family and childbearing.
- **The state follows**: Trump signed an executive order expanding access to IVF (February 2025), apparently in tension with classic anti-IVF conservatism, but consistent with the pronatalist support of the "tech right" that funded his campaign. At Natal Con 2025, figures like Jack Posobiec framed demographic decline as a threat to "national identity," and the presence of white supremacists there is documented (HOPE not hate). The journal Columbia Political Review speaks of the "racial politics of American pronatalism."

The hinge with Part 28 is here, and it is sharp: pronatalism says "have more children," embryo selection says "make the good ones." Together, they form Eugenics 2.0, voluntary and market-based.

## 4. Reactionary Masculinity and Vitalism

Beyond childbearing, there is a culture of virility. The crudest pole is **Bronze Age Pervert** (Costin Alamariu, a Yale PhD), whose *Bronze Age Mindset* (2018) circulated in tech-right and Trumpist circles: an anonymous bodybuilt Nietzscheanism, a cult of physical strength and the "based" male, contempt for the managerial and "feminized" world of HR and compliance. It is the "based," anti-"soy" aesthetic, which rejects the protective-maternal pole.

And this pole has a translation in the dossier's central discourse: in Andreessen's manifesto (Part 1), the named enemies ("safetyism," precaution, "trust and safety") are precisely the function of care and protection, culturally coded as feminine. The "safety" that is erased from the international vocabulary in eighteen months (Part 17) is not a neutral word: it is a gendered word, that of prudence and care, evacuated in favor of the register of conquest and dominance.

## 5. Gender in the Machines Themselves

Gender is not only in the men who lead, it is in the products:

- **Default female assistant voices** (the long history of Siri, Alexa, Cortana) that assign docile service to the feminine.
- **The "AI girlfriends" market** and artificial companions, which industrializes male loneliness and the asymmetric relationship.
- **The gendered labor of AI**: moderation and annotation, that work of "care" and "safety" that makes the models usable, is feminized and underpaid (Part 16). The same extractive layer, reread through gender, reveals that it is largely the work of women of the South that absorbs the violence to make the tool presentable.

## 6. The Counter-Field: Data Feminism, Haraway

So that this is not a one-way indictment, the theoretical counter-field is needed:

- **Catherine D'Ignazio and Lauren Klein, *Data Feminism* (2020)**: a method for reading data systems through power and gender, against the false neutrality of "it computes, it's objective."
- **Donna Haraway, the "Cyborg Manifesto"**: the inverse utopia of the milieu's. Where the tech-right dreams of a hierarchical and virile post-human, Haraway proposes the cyborg as a blurring of borders (human/machine, man/woman), as an anti-essentialist and anti-pure figure. It is the feminist cosmotechnics that the dossier can oppose to the transhumanism of Part 4. *(Haraway is, moreover, among the critical voices available for Ismaël's practice.)*

## 7. Verdict and the Trap to Avoid

1. **Gender is a vector, not a backdrop.** State pronatalism, embryo selection, "based" virilism, the evacuation of "care": these are structural elements of the ideology, and they are the ones that rhyme most directly with historical fascism (natalism + virility). With Part 28, it is the diptych that justifies the word "fascism" better than the purely economic parts.
2. **The trap: do not make it an atmosphere.** As with eugenics, the rule is to document, name, date. Not "masculinist atmosphere" but "second Natal Conference, Austin, 2025, embryo selection via Genomic Prediction, documented presence of supremacists." Precision makes the difference between a thesis and a mood.
3. **The honesty caveat**: not all pronatalists are fascists, and worrying about childbearing is not in itself a crime. What tips it over is the coupling with genetic selection, the aesthetics of dominance, and the "national identity / civilizational decline" framing. It is that precise coupling, not childbearing in general, that the part documents.
