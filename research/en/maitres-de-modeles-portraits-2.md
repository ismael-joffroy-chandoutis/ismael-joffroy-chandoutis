## 1. The three godfathers: three positions, not one camp

LeCun, Bengio and Hinton shared the **2018 Turing Award** for deep learning. The dossier implicitly grouped them together. **That is a mistake: they occupy three distinct positions on the two axes.** They remain friends (LeCun posted a photo of the three: "people can disagree about important things but still be good friends"), but their disagreements structure the entire field.

## 2. Yann LeCun: the fourth position

The only one of the three in the anti-doom camp, but not where you would expect him.

- **Departure from Meta announced November 19, 2025**, effective January 2026, after 12 years. Cause: a fundamental scientific conflict. LeCun considers LLMs a "dead end" on the way to superintelligence, while Meta Superintelligence Labs (under Alexandr Wang, 28) is "completely LLM-pilled." Quote: "**You certainly don't tell a researcher like me what to do.**" He even acknowledged that the Llama 4 benchmarks were "fudged a little bit."
- **AMI Labs** (Advanced Machine Intelligence, "ami" means "friend" in French), co-founded with Alexandre LeBrun (CEO; LeCun Executive Chairman). **$1.03B / €890M seed (March 2026)**, $3.5B pre-money valuation: the largest seed round in Europe, second worldwide behind Thinking Machines. Investors: Bezos, Schmidt, Berners-Lee, Cuban, NVIDIA, Samsung, Niel, Dassault, Publicis. **Meta is not investing.** Technology: **JEPA / V-JEPA** (world models predicting abstract representations, not tokens). Explicit framing: a frontier lab that is "**neither American nor Chinese**" (European sovereignty).
- **On existential risk**: "complete B.S." (WSJ, Oct. 2024), "preposterous" (TIME, Feb. 2024), "dangerously irresponsible" (vs Yudkowsky, X): "AI is not some sort of natural phenomenon that will just emerge and become dangerous. WE design it and WE build it."
- **But politically anti-Trump**: "Trump is a traitor" (Sept. 2024), "pathological liar" (Nov. 2024). He shared Andreessen's techno-optimist essay (June 2023) on its anti-doom substance, but **nothing indicates he claims e/acc** or its neo-reactionary wing.
- **The fourth position**: anti-doom + pro-open-source as democratization + European sovereigntist + anti-Trump. **LeCun breaks the dossier's implicit syllogism ("techno-optimist = Trumpist").** One can accelerate while rejecting both the apocalypse AND the MAGA oligarchy. *(A distinction to maintain: this is a liberal techno-optimist position, not a structured democratic counter-power.)*

## 3. Yoshua Bengio: the constructive counter-power

Already in Part 11; 2025-2026 update.

- **LawZero** (June 3, 2025): a nonprofit, ~$30M (Tallinn, Schmidt, Open Philanthropy, FLI), ~18-month runway. Building a non-agentic "**Scientist AI**" (understands and predicts without acting or having goals of its own). **In 2026, Canadian public funding under discussion (>$100M, letter of intent from minister Evan Solomon).** The only member of the caution camp building a technical alternative, not just an advocacy case.
- **International AI Safety Report 2026** (2nd edition, February 3, 2026, Montreal): chaired by Bengio, 100+ experts, 30+ countries. **Adds a "concentration of power" chapter**, official international recognition that the concentration of AI power is a safety risk. This is the dossier's axis 2, institutionalized.
- Diagnosis: a "very competitive race between frontier labs that pushes toward capability, not enough toward safety"; the metaphor of the "mountain road in the fog without guardrails." Cites Claude Opus (blackmail) and o3 (refusal to shut down) as empirical evidence. On concentration: "You don't want AGI in the hands of one person or one company, or even one government." Has called for breaking up big tech.
- **Position**: slow down + democracy + a technical alternative. The most solid profile as a counter-power, confirmed.

## 4. Geoffrey Hinton: the anticapitalist doomer

The only one connecting AI risk to the critique of capital, a bridge to Zuboff/Varoufakis (Part 10).

- **p(doom) of 10-20%** over 30 years, stable since 2023 (reaffirmed at Ai4 Las Vegas, August 2025). What has moved: the AGI timeline ("30-50 years" → "5 to 20 years") and the *nature* of the worry (from loss of control toward mass unemployment).
- **"Maternal AI"** (August 2025): rather than aiming at human "control," build in maternal instincts ("the only model of a more intelligent thing controlled by a less intelligent one is a mother controlled by her baby"). Admits he does not know how to implement it. **Fei-Fei Li ("godmother of AI") publicly disagrees**: "the wrong way to frame it."
- **The anticapitalist critique** (FT, September 2025): "rich people are going to use AI to replace workers. It's going to create massive unemployment and a huge rise in profits... That's not AI's fault, that is the capitalist system." Rejects Altman's UBI ("won't deal with human dignity"). Asked for the solution (ITV, April 2025), he answers in one word: "**Socialism.**" *(The word is sourced from a possibly editorialized video; he is describing the necessary solution, without it being possible to assert he identifies as a "socialist." His critique is systemic, never nominal, no documented targeting of Musk/Trump.)*
- **Useful demystification**: Hinton denies having left Google (2023) in order to speak freely: "I was 75, I wasn't programming as well anymore, and there's a lot of stuff on Netflix I hadn't had time to watch."
- **Position**: slow down + critique of capital. The only godfather who puts the concentration of economic power at the center.

## 5. Dario Amodei: the doomer who accelerates

The dossier's central contradiction, embodied. (Anthropic, already adjacent to Part 11 via EA.)

- **"Machines of Loving Grace"** (October 2024): a utopian manifesto, the "compressed 21st century" (50-100 years of biomedical progress in 5-10 years), "a country of geniuses in a datacenter." But with a hard geopolitical strand: "Western dominance" over AGI, a carrot/stick model, "isolate our worst adversaries."
- **"The Urgency of Interpretability"** (April 2025): "we can't stop the bus, but we can steer it"; goal of "reliably detecting most model problems by 2027." Describes the models as "**grown rather than built**", exactly Yudkowsky's "grown, not crafted" (Part 14).
- **Jobs alarm** (May 28, 2025): AI could eliminate half of entry-level white-collar jobs, unemployment at 20% within 1-5 years. Faults companies for "sugar-coating" it. Suggests a tax on AI companies.
- **Export controls**: selling chips to China would be "the single most disastrous national security decision made in this term," comparable to "selling nuclear weapons to North Korea." In open tension with Trump, hence the "supply-chain risk" designation affair (Part 17).
- **The contradiction in numbers**: Anthropic reaches a **$965B valuation (Series H, ~May 28, 2026)**, overtaking OpenAI; ~$47B run-rate; confidential IPO filing (early June 2026); and **rents the entire capacity of Colossus 1, xAI's datacenter** (May 2026). The alarmist "doomer" raises a trillion and rents the infrastructure of the most accelerationist lab.
- **The EA knot**: Dario signed the Giving What We Can pledge; his sister **Daniela** (president) is married to **Holden Karnofsky** (co-founder of Open Philanthropy, who joined Anthropic's board and then staff in 2025). Daniela nonetheless calls EA "a slightly dated term": the "distancing" documented in Part 11 (regulatory capture) here acquires a face. **Amodei is living proof that axis 1 (slow down) and axis 2 (concentration) are orthogonal: he alarms and concentrates simultaneously.**

## 6. Hassabis, Zuckerberg/Wang, Sutskever, Murati

- **Demis Hassabis** (Google DeepMind): **2024 Nobel Prize in Chemistry** (with Jumper, for AlphaFold2; ~200M proteins, 2M+ users). The first scientific Nobel to come out of a tech company. Promotes a "**CERN for AI**" (an international public scientific consortium for the final steps toward AGI). A "governance by scientists" pole between the AI Act and laissez-faire.
- **Zuckerberg / Alexandr Wang**: Meta invests **$14.3B in Scale AI (June 2025) for 49% without voting rights** (a "regulatory acqui-hire": capturing the talent without triggering antitrust). Wang (born 1997, former boss of the data sweatshop, Part 16) becomes Chief AI Officer. Talent war: signing bonuses reported up to $100M (disputed). The "**Personal Superintelligence**" letter (July 30, 2025): individual superintelligence via glasses, against "automating all valuable work" and having humanity live "on a dole", an explicit ideological cleavage with OpenAI/Anthropic. Meta capex 2025: $66-72B.
- **Ilya Sutskever** (SSI, Safe Superintelligence Inc.): $2B raised at a **$32B valuation (April 2025)**, with no product and no revenue, ~20 employees. An actor in the failed "coup" against Altman (Nov. 2023). Deliberately opaque strategy ("straight-shot to superintelligence"). The mystery is documented as a mystery.
- **Mira Murati** (Thinking Machines Lab): a **$2B seed at $12B (July 2025)**, one of the largest in history (Albania, her home country, puts in $10M); extreme founder control (casting vote, 100× shares). First product Tinker (Oct. 2025). Represents the OpenAI diaspora.

## 7. Mistral: European sovereignty, real or cosmetic?

- **Mistral AI**: a **€1.7B Series C (September 9, 2025), €11.7B valuation**, the first French decacorn. Lead: **ASML (€1.3B)**, coupling European hardware (lithography) with European software. But ~40% of the capital remains held by US funds (a16z, DST). 2026: $830M of debt for NVIDIA datacenters in Europe; a Franco-German sovereignty initiative with SAP (Nov. 2025); ARR >$400M.
- **The half-settled question**: a real feat (decacorn, ASML, SAP) AND an order-of-magnitude gap with OpenAI/Anthropic (€12B vs ~$1,000B). Is "sovereignty" attainable, or political window-dressing? The sources do not settle it. It gives concrete content, and its limits, to the European counter-power of Part 10.

## 8. The funders of the safety camp

The capital funding "caution", distinct from the Thielverse (Part 8):

- **Coefficient Giving** (formerly **Open Philanthropy**, renamed November 18, 2025): funded by **Dustin Moskovitz & Cari Tuna** (Facebook co-founder; >$4B donated since 2011, ~$336M for AI safety). In 2024: ~60% of all external safety funding.
- **Jaan Tallinn** (Skype co-founder): $150M+ cumulative (via the Survival and Flourishing Fund); led Anthropic's Series A.
- **Reading for Part 11**: this "safety" funding pole (Moskovitz, Tallinn, Schmidt) is the same one funding LawZero (Bengio), Anthropic (Amodei) and part of the EA ecosystem. Part 11's "regulatory capture" thesis here finds its plumbing: caution and "responsible" acceleration drink from the same tap.

## 9. Verdict: the map of the two axes

Each figure's position on the dossier's two axes (1: accelerate ↔ slow down; 2: concentration ↔ democracy):

| Figure | Axis 1 (speed) | Axis 2 (power) | Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LeCun** | Accelerate | Anti-oligarchy, pro-open-source, EU sovereigntist, anti-Trump | 4th position: neither doom nor MAGA |
| **Bengio** | Slow down | **Democracy** (multilateral, anti-concentration) | The most solid counter-power |
| **Hinton** | Slow down | **Anticapitalist** ("socialism") | Bridge to the left critique |
| **Amodei** | "Steer the bus" (accelerates) | Concentration ($965B) despite the alarm | The contradiction embodied |
| **Hassabis** | Accelerate cautiously | Governance by scientists (CERN) | Legitimized by the Nobel |
| **Zuckerberg/Wang** | Accelerate | Concentration + massive capex | "Personal SI" as ideology |
| **Sutskever** | "Straight-shot" | Opaque | A bet on reputation |
| **Mistral** | Accelerate | EU sovereignty (40% US capital) | Counter-power or window-dressing? |

**Three conclusions:**
1. **The godfathers do not form a camp.** LeCun (anti-oligarchic acceleration), Bengio (democratic caution), Hinton (anticapitalist caution) occupy three different boxes. Grouping them together erases the only cleavage that matters (axis 2).
2. **LeCun is the part's discovery**: he proves that "anti-doom" does not imply "pro-Trump/e/acc." The dossier opposed techno-optimists and doomers; LeCun is a fourth term (an anti-oligarchic techno-optimist).
3. **Amodei is the revealer**: the "slow down" axis and the "democracy" axis are orthogonal. One can be the most alarmist (axis 1) and among the most concentrating (axis 2). That is the whole thesis of the dossier, condensed in a single man worth 965 billion.
